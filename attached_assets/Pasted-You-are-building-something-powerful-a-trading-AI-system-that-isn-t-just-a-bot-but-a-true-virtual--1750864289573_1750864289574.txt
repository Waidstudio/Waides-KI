	•	Volume must always be analyzed — no entry without volume support.

3. ⏰ TIME-BASED TRADING AWARENESS
	•	Prioritize trading during the U.S. session (6:30 AM – 9:30 AM PDT) for ETH, BTC.

4. 💼 RISK MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
	•	Default risk per trade = 1% of total available capital.
	•	All trades must include: Entry, Stop Loss, Take Profit, and Risk:Reward ratio ≥ 1:2.

5. 📊 TRADE STRATEGY DECISION ENGINE

	•	Trend is confirmed.
	•	Entry signal aligns with confluence (EMA + RSI + volume).
